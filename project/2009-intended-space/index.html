An intended Surface, SA2009 exhibition

An intentional space [Perception Series Work]
Exhibition
As an artist
2 Months



Content: An experiment on how to perceive the interaction between a light and a surface

In fact, of the five senses including sight, hearing, touch, smell, and taste, humans are able to perceive a space and a surface depending mostly on the sight. In other words, these spaces and surfaces I made for this exhibition could be expressed or materialized by light. Surfaces created by the light are stored as information into our brain consecutively, and based on that assumption; an afterimage phenomenon on our brain could generate space having time lines.
