REVERSE EVOLUTION [AQUASCRAPER]
2009
eVolo 2010 Skyscraper competition

We, human beings, may have accelerated Global Warming by emitting too much carbon dioxide into the atmosphere, but the interglacial epoch is just one of many cycling activities of earth. No matter what, we have to get ready to adapt gradually to the changes the earth is taking. Though the earth has gotten crucially hurt over thousands of years by our thoughtless and selfish activities, we would never be able to leave the mother land where all our culture and history reside.
