Exhibition: Politics of Space and its Shadows


Seoul, Korea, Sep 2 - Nov 5, 2017.

Dongsei Kim's work with Namju Lee, Politics of Shade and its Shadows was invited to the Seoul Biennale International Studios (SBIS) exhibition curated by John Hong (SNU)
at the inaugural Seoul Biennale of Architecture and Urbanism directed by Hyungmin Pai and Alejandro Zaera-Polo.
