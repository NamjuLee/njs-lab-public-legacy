Algorithmic Surface [Series Work]
2009
Collaboration with director, Janga Park
HD 1024 x 576
3 Months

Invisible Dream is a short film. In this project, we worked
as Background Director and my goal was to design virtual scenes
for recording film with actors.
The virtual world is derived from the actor’s mind and experiences.
Thus, I tried to reflect the actor’s subconscious
in order to design a city including buildings.
