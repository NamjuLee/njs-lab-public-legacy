KOREAN TRADITIONAL HOUSE, ARCHITECTURAL COLLAGE

Exhibition
2010
3 Months

This is a visualization work submitted to an architectural
exhibition, which was inspired by a traditional Korean building.
By digitally reconstructing the building process and overlapping it to the actual context,
this work suggests reminders to visitors regarding laborious construction endeavors,
beyond the state where they passively appreciate architectural externals.
